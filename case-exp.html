Case Study · Charity Livestream

EXP: Every eXtra Penny

A cross-brand charity livestream bringing together talent from the Capitals, Wizards, Mystics, and Team Liquid to raise money for the Boys & Girls Clubs.

Challenge

Create a charity moment that brought multiple Monumental brands and outside talent together for a real cause, not just cross-promotion.

Approach

Conceptualized and produced a cross-brand charity livestream, coordinating talent, scheduling, and production across four different organizations.

Result

Raised over $1,500 for the Boys & Girls Clubs while strengthening ties across the organization's brands and outside partners.
